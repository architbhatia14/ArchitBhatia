Hello, Welcome to my page

Archit Bhatia is a 4th year Computer Systems Engineering Student at Carleton University. From a very young age he became passionate about computers and their functionality, this led him to pursuing his degree in Computer Engineering. He is currently looking for a job opportunity where he could bring his skills and knowledge to action.

Throughout his time at Carleton he had worked on many group projects which included research and development. During his 2nd year he worked in a group to research a safety layer to be applied in a hydrogen tank, he later presented the findings while explaining how the technology worked.

3rd year he worked on a Smart Shelf project to maximize the efficiency of stocking products on retail shelfs. For this project he worked on the GUI application, developing a basic user-friendly interface on an Android device using Android Studio. Later this application was connected to a database to add the user's information to the main database platform.

During the summer he works on personal projects, the most recent one being creating a website to learn basic HTML. This website was composed of different effects such as hovers and transitions which was used to display the skills learned throughout the project.
